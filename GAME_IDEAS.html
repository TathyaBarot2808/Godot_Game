Full Enemy Roster (5 Enemy Types)
You currently have Flying Eye and Green Slime sprites. Build 5 distinct enemy archetypes that force different playstyles:

1. Stalker — ground enemy that chases the player, vulnerable to recoil knockback
2. Anchor — stationary turret that shoots at the player, must dash-dodge its projectiles
3. Charger — lunges across the room when player is in line-of-sight, punishes standing still

5. Bouncer — flying enemy that ricochets off walls, creates chaos in enclosed rooms
